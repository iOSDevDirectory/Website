# What is the iOS Dev Directory?

What if there was a place you could go to find a comprehensive list of blogs & sites on all aspects of iOS app development? Well, now there is!

This list of sites was originally put together, and is maintained by [Dave Verwer](https://daveverwer.com). I seeded it with a couple of hundred blogs that I was already watching and built a quick site around it to make it a bit more attractive. Finally, I put something together to generate OPML files for each category so you can quickly and easily follow everything yourself, if you'd like to!

I hope you find the site useful!

Dave
