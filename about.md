# What is the iOS Dev Directory?

What if there was a place you could go to find a comprehensive list of blogs & sites on all aspects of iOS app development? Well, now there is!

This list of sites was originally put together, and is maintained by [Dave Verwer](https://twitter.com/daveverwer). I seeded it with a couple of hundred blogs that I was already watching and built a quick site around it to make it a bit more attractive. Finally, I put something together to generate OPML files for each category so you can quickly and easily follow everything yourself, if you'd like to!

Of course, if you don't have the time to follow *everything* yourself then the best way to keep up to date with the best content published by this wonderful community is to [subscribe to iOS Dev Weekly](https://iosdevweekly.com). It's a round up of all the best links about iOS development, published every Friday for free.

I hope you find the site useful!

Dave
