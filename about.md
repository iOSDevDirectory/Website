# What is the iOS Dev Directory?

What if there was a place you could go to find a comprehensive list of blogs & sites on all aspects of iOS app development? Well, now there is!

This list was originally built, and is maintained by [Dave Verwer](https://twitter.com/daveverwer). I originally seeded the list with a couple of hundred blogs that I was already watching and then built a quick site around it to make it a bit more attractive and automatically generate OPML files so you can quickly and easily follow everything yourself.

Of course, if you don't want to follow *everything* yourself then the best way to keep up to date with the best content published by this wonderful community is to [subscribe to iOS Dev Weekly](https://iosdevweekly.com). It's a round up of all the best links about iOS development, published every Friday for free.

I hope you find the site useful!

Dave
