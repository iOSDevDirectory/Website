This page probably doesn't contain enough legal jargon to be fully compliant but this is what the iOS Dev Directory does in terms of privacy and cookies.

# Privacy

The listings on this site are entirely generated from the JSON files in [this repository](https://github.com/daveverwer/iOSDevDirectory/). If you are the owner of a site which is listed here and would like it to be removed, please let me know by [opening an issue](https://github.com/daveverwer/iOSDevDirectory/issues/new).

# Cookies

[Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/) is used to to gather basic analytics data. No other analytics, trackers or cookies are used.
